Photo Upload

The next sections are related to photo upload and linking. Click Upload Assets to enter the upload screen, follow along on how to upload a photo. 

Remember to follow the naming convention: PIMID-VendorNumber_01.jpg. The PIMID-VendorNumber is always present at the top and can be copied to the photo name, just add _01 to the end, _02 for the 2nd photos and so one.
